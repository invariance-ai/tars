# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the tars community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Taking responsibility, apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, publishing
others' private information without permission, and other conduct that would reasonably be
considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainers at conduct@invariance.ai. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
